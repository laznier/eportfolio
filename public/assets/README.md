# Assets Folder

Drop your files here and then tell me what to wire up.

## Subfolders

| Folder | What goes here |
|--------|---------------|
| `pdfs/` | PDF documents — analysis papers, reports, briefings |
| `slides/` | PowerPoint or PDF slide decks |
| `images/` | Charts, diagrams, screenshots, SWOT/PESTEL/Five Forces visuals |
| `artifacts/` | Course deliverables — any format |

## How to wire them in

1. Drop your files into the appropriate subfolder
2. Give me a list of what you added and which page/section each one belongs to
3. I'll link them into the site

Files placed here will be served at `/assets/...` on the live site.
For example: `assets/pdfs/tesla-analysis.pdf` → `https://yoursite.com/assets/pdfs/tesla-analysis.pdf`
